Account & Data Deletion Request

How to Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email from the address associated with your Dhobi Kart account to: kartdhobi@gmail.com
  2. Use the following subject line for your email:
    Account Deletion Request
  3. In the body of the email, please include your **full name** and the **phone number** registered with your account to help us verify your identity.

Our support team will process your request and confirm the deletion via email within 7-14 business days.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon the successful processing of your deletion request, the following personally identifiable information associated with your account will be permanently removed from our production servers:

  • Personal Profile Information: Your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Saved Addresses: All pickup and delivery addresses you have saved to your profile.
  • Order History: All past and current order details, including services requested and transaction amounts.
  • Authentication Data: Your login credentials and session tokens.

Data Retention

For legal or legitimate business purposes, such as financial auditing or fraud prevention, we may retain anonymized and non-personally identifiable transaction data for a period of up to 2 years. This data will be fully dissociated from your personal account and cannot be used to identify you.
